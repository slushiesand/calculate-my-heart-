<h1> Calculate My Heart! </h1>
A math platformer where you differentiate and integrate various monomials to progress through a visual novel.

<img width="1250" height="692" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-17 003914" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6abe0f92-f511-4660-b8ce-181988f10fcc" />

<i>
  <p> In Mathemetopia, all Functions and Operations search for the way to become "clear," to make their world a better place. They obey the laws set in stone by Newton and Leibniz, to live their lives as free as they please. </p>
  <p> But what is "freedom," if not decided by you? </p>
  <p> (... nah, the game isn't that serious. i just got a little carried away with writing.) </p>
</i>

https://slushiesand.itch.io/calculate-my-heart
<br> For HackClub's Calculate.

<ul>
  <li> Eight levels slowly progressing in difficulty -- Two being timed. </li>
  <li> Hand-drawn pixel sprites and portraits. </li>
  <li> Math metaphors. </li>
</ul>
